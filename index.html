About Bug Eyed Technologies

Bug Eyed Technologies is a small, dedicated IT consulting business specializing in entry-level IT solutions for individuals and small businesses.

Our Mission: To provide accessible, reliable, and professional IT services that help small businesses thrive in the digital age.

Founded by cybersecurity professional Tyler Burke, we bring military intelligence analytical skills and cutting-edge technical expertise to every project. Whether you're setting up your first network or need ongoing IT support, we're here to help.
